    • Mice

    I originally bought the mice as I went with one of my best friends to buy more pet mice since two of hers had passed away and she only had one left. Mice are very social so we had visited a nearby petshop and I saw one male mouse that had the most interesting patterns. Due to her mouse at the time being a female, we needed to buy more females and weren't able to buy a male. We chose two other female mice and had left the petshop. The following day, I was very interested in purchasing the male mouse that we had seen, I had asked my best friend to see whether he was still available. After the phone call to the petshop, she had told me that he had already been sold and I can understand why he was very popular. At this point, I wanted to buy pet mice and she still wanted to buy a hairless one. I called various petshops over the next few days and had visited a different one to the previous petshop which had mice that were only a month old. I had bought three being told and thinking that they were all males. Later that night, I realised that one is a female and I didn't have another cage to separate her from her brothers. At this point, I had Jimmy-David, Julius Cheeser and Eugenie. The same best friend, another friend and I named these mice.

    The next day, I had already grown attached to the Eugenie so I decided to get her a friend/sister since I'm not sure whether the new mouse was from the same family. I bought the second female mouse, a second cage and the essentials for this cage. A different friend named this new female mouse, Vapi which is short for my favourite restaurant, Vapiano.

    After looking on Gumtree, I had found a hairless mouse for my best friend, we had decided to pick up the hairless mouse a few days later. After this visit which was an hour away, my best friend was going home with a fourth mouse and I was going home with my fifth and sixth mice which are Cutie and Houdini. My mum had named Cutie and another friend, different to the previous friend had named Houdini.

    Kittens

    For a few months, I had wanted to purchase a kitten for my mum but I hadn't looked for any until earlier this year. When looking I had found it hard to find a rescue kitten that would be suitable and was actually available. After approximately a month of looking, I had enquired about a kitten but it had just been adopted. The lady that replied to my enquiry was able to provide information about a few kittens that were going to be available to adopt approximately two weeks later, but were only nine weeks at the time. Two weeks later, after Charlotte had fully recovered from surgery, I drove an hour with my best friend and her sister to pick Charlotte up. After picking her up, we had surprised my mum with her new kitten. She was definitely surprised and kind of didn't believe that she now had a kitten. She was very happy with Charlotte after the shock wore off. Charlotte made herself at home very quickly and is exactly the way they had described her in the information provided. Charlotte is a gorgeous and wonderful kitten who seemed to have been slightly bored after leaving a home with multiple cats and kittens. I had seen a post about a gorgeous blue eyed kitten and had messaged the page to see whether it would be available.
