Welcome to my blog about Auslan!

Deafblind Victoria (2021)

As displayed in the Auslan blog preview, I have completed two Introduction to Auslan courses and am also looking forward to continuing my learning after I have completed this Web Development bootcamp!

The picture above shows the Auslan Alphabet which is what I habe learnt amongst other things sunch as numbers, grocery items amongst other things.

The courses that I completed have the option to be online or in-person. I'm fortunate to live close to one of the in-person facilities. When learning Auslan, it is recommended to attend events for the deaf/hard of hearing community to be exposed to the language and culture more. This will increase knowledge and understaning in so many areas.

If you are interested, I do recommend starting to learn some Auslan signs, you might've not noticed that you've already used a few before!
