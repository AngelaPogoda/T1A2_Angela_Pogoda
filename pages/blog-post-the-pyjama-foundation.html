The Pyjama Foundation (2022)

In my last job, we had celebrated Pyjama Day in the office, this raised awareness for The Pyjama Foundation. I remember in primary and high school that we were able to donate to The Pyjama Foundation. At the time, I didn't understand what it was. Recently, I had gathered more information, some from my colleague and the rest from The Pyjama Foundation's website or throughout the application process. One of the goals of a Pyjama Angel is to help the Pyjama Child learn to read better. Another is for the Pyjama Angel to be a consistent part of the Pyjama Child's life for the time that you're visiting them, especially since the main goal is to reunite the child with their parents.

I have found that being a Pyjama Angel is incredibly rewarding and I'm excited to attend every visit just like I was the first.

To find out more, please see more information on The Pyjama Foundations website.
